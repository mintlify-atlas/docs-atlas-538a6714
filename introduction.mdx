## What is Ayuntamiento de Zongolica?

The **Ayuntamiento de Zongolica** is a comprehensive digital platform that serves as the official government portal and tourism system for the municipality of Zongolica, Veracruz, Mexico. This modern web application bridges government services with cultural preservation and tourism promotion.

## Key Capabilities

The platform combines two core systems:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Government Portal" icon="landmark">
    Access municipal services, institutional information, transparency documents, and administrative procedures all in one place.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tourism System" icon="compass">
    Discover Zongolica's natural wonders through personalized recommendations, interactive onboarding, and gamified exploration.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Government Services

- **Institutional Information** - Learn about the municipal president, council members, and government structure
- **Municipal Services** - Access online procedures, permits, and administrative services
- **Transparency** - Download official documents, reports, and public information
- **Directory** - Contact information for government officials and departments
- **Call-to-Action Pages** - Important announcements and citizen alerts

### Tourism Platform

- **Xochitlanis** - Interactive tourism onboarding with personalized route recommendations
- **17 Tourist Attractions** - Comprehensive guide to waterfalls, caves, viewpoints, and cultural sites
- **Badge Gamification** - Unlock achievements by visiting attractions and completing challenges
- **Ticket Generation** - Create shareable visual tickets with personalized routes
- **User Profiles** - Save favorites, track visits, and build your travel history

## Project Context

Zongolica is located in the mountainous region of central Veracruz, Mexico, known for its:

- **Natural Beauty** - Waterfalls, caves, natural arches, and pristine rivers
- **Indigenous Culture** - Strong Nahuatl-speaking community with preserved traditions
- **Adventure Tourism** - Rappelling, caving, hiking, and eco-tourism opportunities
- **Religious Heritage** - Colonial-era churches and sacred sites

This platform helps preserve and promote Zongolica's unique identity while modernizing government services for its citizens.
